COVID-19 SELF-SCREENING QUESTIONNAIRE

All staff must complete before beginning their work shift or entering the workplace.

  1. Do you have any of the following new or worsening symptoms or signs?

  2. Has a doctor, health care provider, or public health unit told you that you should currently be isolating (staying at home)?

  3. Have you been identified as a "close contact" of someone who currently has COVID-19 in the last 14 days?

  4. Have you received a COVID Alert exposure notification on your cell phone in the last 14 days (and have not been tested or waiting for your result)?

  5. Have you or anyone you live with traveled outside of Canada within the last 14 days, and you have not been fully vaccinated (2nd dose) for more than 14 days?
